What is the Architecture, Engineering & Construction (AEC) Collection ?

The AEC Collection bundles all of the top Autodesk design products with 12 additional products for design, analysis, visualization, reality capture, detailing and automation. The AEC Collection provides a full set of BIM and CAD tools supported by a cloud-based common data environment that facilitates project delivery from conception through to construction. By bundling these top products: Revit, Civil 3D, AutoCAD, Navisworks, Infaworks with all the additional products, customers save over $8000
